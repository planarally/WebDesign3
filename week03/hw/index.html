about us

In this crazy world today, we are each seeking a path towards growth and enlightenment, and we believe your journey ought to include the simple pleasures, like donuts. But why eat greasy fried donuts when you can indulge in a more evolved creation? fōnuts are the delicious, better-for-you treat always baked fresh daily, and served with a healthy dose of loving kindness. Treat yourself better and eat a fōnut!

fōnuts creator Nancy Truman and her husband Thom Furtado have embraced this evolution and transformed all your favorite flavors to 100% gluten-free, with low-to-no sugar added…while always expanding the popular new seasonal and vegan flavors.

The origin of fōnuts…

As the story goes, it all started with a cup of coffee, and the need for something yummy to go with it! L.A. native Nancy Truman always loved pairing her morning and, okay, afternoon, coffee with baked goods. But after discovering her own gluten intolerance, she realized how hard it was to find a quality, all-natural gluten-free treat. All the gf snacks out there were either too sweet, or had the consistency of dirt, and usually both!

Her original goal was simple: create something that her taste buds and stomach would love. Recalling her childhood memories of her mother Samyra’s baking for the neighborhood, Nancy found she could honor her mother by transforming some of Samyra’s recipes into healthier gluten-free versions. In early 2011, while selling her first gluten-free, low-sugar product Banancy Bread at the Mar Vista Farmer’s Market, Nancy had her epiphany, “Oh, I could use donut baking pans to make a cute, fun, faux-donut!”

From this humble beginning, the fōnut was born… the beloved old-fashioned cake donut reinvented as a modern classic. Always baked, never fried, emphasizing simple recipes, all-natural, gluten-free ingredients, served with a healthy dose of loving kindness.

That summer, the first fōnut shop opened to critical acclaim and widespread success. Envisioned as a fresh take on the timeless donut shop experience we all grew up with, fōnuts quickly became LA’s go-to destination for anyone seeking a “better-for-you” donut.
