New Designs...

...Legacy Tech|

From Pong to Final Fantasy, gaming has made its way into our hearts and homes rather comfortably over the last few decades. However, as new consoles are released, older machines and consoles have taken a backseat... until OKEDO.

We have the experience to take your old consoles and turn them into something new, as well as make a few designs of our own, whether it's a custom arcade cabinet, a gameboy-turned-midi controller, or a whole videogame itself.

In a world that is realizing the importance of sustainability, we aim to apply it to the world of gaming. Come realize your gaming dream with us!
CORE VALUES

Innovation

Our well trained programmers and engineers have a new way of thinking about games, using the traditions of old. Applying old designs to new technology has paved the way for our corporate clients' influence to grow.

Creativity

We believe games are an art form, whether it's the design on the cabinet, the beautiful math of the programming, or the color of the pixels themselves. Our team of artists work at creating something unique and beautiful every time.

Sustainability

There are more than a few SNES controllers floating in our ocean by now. Gaming should not be a wasteful industry, and we aim to put a significant dent in the amount of plastics in our ocean by reusing and recycling old machinery to create something amazing.
