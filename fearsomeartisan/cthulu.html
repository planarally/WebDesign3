Bottled Percentiles

$10 (On Sale!)

Are you investigating the occult as you slowly lose your sanity at the whims of dice and Eldritch gods? Then this may be the pin for you! These acrylic pins feature original art by me of bottled percentile dice and tiny little Cthulhu-themed monsters, drawn while poking around Arkham on my own horror-filled adventure. Pins are professionally manufactured, about 1 inch on their longest side, and come with a rubber backstop. The parts of the pin around the floating contents are clear, and have a very cool depth effect. I add a layer of resin to the back of each pin for durability. Pins come on a little backing card with a mystery sticker.
